About Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man (チェンソーマン Chensōman?) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuki Fujimoto. The series is divided into two parts, the Public Safety Saga and the Academy Saga. The Public Safety Saga, which contains 97 chapters, follows Denji as the main protagonist. The ongoing Academy Saga introduces a new main protagonist, Asa Mitaka. Serialization of the Public Safety Saga began on December 3, 2018 and concluded on December 14, 2020. An anime adaptation by studio MAPPA adapted the first part of the series up to the Katana Man arc in its first season. The Academy Saga began serialization on July 13, 2022. The series alternates between a weekly and biweekly schedule.

Creators and Publishers

Mangaka: Tatsuki Fujimoto

Tatsuki Fujimoto is the talented Japanese mangaka who created and illustrated Chainsaw Man. Known for his unique storytelling and dark themes, he has also worked on other series like Fire Punch. The manga began serialization in 2018.

Manga Publishers

  • Shueisha: The original Japanese publisher, releasing Chainsaw Man in Weekly Shonen Jump magazine.
  • Viz Media: Handles English translation and distribution in North America, publishing through Shonen Jump.
  • Shueisha (Global): Oversees international releases and digital platforms.

Anime Publishers and Distributors

  • MAPPA: The animation studio responsible for producing the anime adaptation, known for high-quality visuals and action sequences.
  • Crunchyroll: Primary streaming platform for global distribution of the anime seasons.
  • Netflix: Distributes the anime in certain regions, including the movie.

Key Characters

デンジ

Denji is the main protagonist of the manga and anime series Chainsaw Man. He is a teenage Devil Hunter who, after being betrayed and killed by the Yakuza, merges with his pet devil, Pochita, to become a "Hybrid" known as Chainsaw Man.

マキマ

Makima is the enigmatic, manipulative main antagonist of Chainsaw Man's Public Safety Saga. She was the Chief Cabinet Secretary's Personal Devil Hunter who took Denji in as her human pet.

早川アキ

Aki is the deuteragonist of the Public Safety Saga. He was a Public Safety Devil Hunter working under Makima's special squad, who had contracts with the Curse Devil and Future Devil, and a former contract with the Fox Devil.

パワー

Power is the tritagonist of the Public Safety Saga. She is the Blood Fiend who embodies the concept of blood and a Public Safety Devil Hunter working under Makima's special squad.

レゼ

Reze also known as Bomb Girl, is the Bomb Devil Hybrid from the Soviet Union, who was sent to Japan with the mission of stealing the Chainsaw Man's heart. She serves as the titular main antagonist of the Bomb Girl arc and a minor antagonist in the Control Devil arc.

天使の悪魔

Angel Devil is a devil who embodies the concept of angels, and a Public Safety Devil Hunter from Tokyo Special Division 4. He serves as a supporting character in the Public Safety Saga.
